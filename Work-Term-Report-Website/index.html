Work Term Report

Introduction

Hi I am Anish Kapoor 3rd year Software Engineering Student at University of Guelph .This was my first co-op work term at Ciena as an Optical Component Test Engineer. I am creating this website to share my experience and the goals I achieved during my time there. The website outlines my role, learning outcomes, and how this work term has contributed to my professional growth.

Information about the Employer

I worked at Ciena's Ottawa office, which is the largest of the company's locations worldwide. Ciena is a global leader in optical networking, providing networking systems, services, and software that support high-speed and secure data transmission. During my work term, I was part of the New Product Introduction (NPI) team, where I collaborated with various internal teams and even had the opportunity to participate in client meetings, including with clients from Thailand. My main responsibility involved writing code, primarily in C#, to develop production tasks that enhanced the efficiency of internal processes. The experience allowed me to gain hands-on technical skills, while also broadening my exposure to team collaboration and client interactions, making it a highly rewarding experience overall.

Goals

  • Communication Skills: My goal was to improve communication by better understanding tasks given to me, asking clarifying questions, and seeking feedback from my team. By the end of the work term, I became more confident in understanding and explaining tasks.
  • Problem-Solving: I aimed to improve my problem-solving skills by tackling coding tasks efficiently under tight deadlines. With feedback and continuous practice, I became faster and more effective at solving problems.
  • In-Depth Understanding: I wanted to gain a deeper understanding of tasks by analyzing problems and going through documentation. By regularly asking questions and collaborating with team members, I was able to increase my comprehension and solve tasks more easily.
  • Teamwork: As this was my first time working in a large professional team, I set a goal to adapt to the professional environment by observing and learning from my colleagues. By the end of the term, I significantly improved my ability to work in a team and act professionally in meetings.
  • Creative Problem-Solving: My goal was to not only solve problems efficiently but also creatively. With constant feedback and brainstorming, I became better at finding innovative and efficient solutions to coding challenges.

Yes, I did develop goals related to my job tasks. I gained significant experience with coding in C# and became proficient with Ciena's built-in tools for editing tasks and developing new solutions. My focus was on improving my ability to understand large codebases quickly, which will benefit my future work experiences by enabling me to solve more complex problems and collaborate effectively with diverse teams. While I made substantial progress, I still aim to enhance my skills further in understanding large-scale code to continue creating innovative solutions.

Job Description

During my co-op term at Ciena, I worked as an Optical Component Test Engineer. My primary responsibilities involved writing and maintaining code used in production systems. I also supported other teams, particularly when one of the leads was on leave, by troubleshooting and fixing errors in the production code. This role provided me with valuable insights into how Ciena manages and processes data, and how their internal systems operate. The skills required for this job included teamwork and proficiency in C#, a language I had a basic understanding of but learned rapidly on the job. My experience with teamwork was built during my second year at university, which proved essential in adapting to the collaborative work environment at Ciena.

Conclusions

In conclusion, my co-op work term at Ciena was an incredibly valuable experience. I gained substantial insights into working in a professional environment and collaborating effectively with a team. I am grateful for the opportunity to develop my skills in coding, problem-solving, and communication. If asked to describe the contents of this website, I would want the audience to recognize it as a reflection of my growth and achievements during this work term, highlighting the skills I acquired and the contributions I made to the team at Ciena.

Acknowledgments

I would like to extend my sincere thanks to my manager, Kevin Morlang, and the entire team I worked with during my co-op term at Ciena. Their support and guidance were invaluable, and I greatly appreciate the opportunity to learn and grow under their mentorship. It was a fantastic experience, and I am grateful for the knowledge and skills I acquired with their help.
